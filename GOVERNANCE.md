# Governance Model: SRO Unincorporated

**CivicConnect** is governed by **SRO Unincorporated**, a decentralized citizen collective. This document outlines our transparency-first approach to managing a public service without a formal corporate structure.

## 1. Core Principles
* **Neutrality:** The system must remain a "Digital Postman." No governance decision shall allow for the filtering or modification of citizen grievances based on content.
* **Non-Commerciality:** The entity shall never introduce advertisements, subscription fees, or data-monetization schemes.
* **Privacy by Default:** All technical updates must prioritize the **DPDP Act 2023** standards, ensuring data is stored locally in JSON and is easily erasable.

## 2. Decision-Making Process
As an unincorporated entity, decisions regarding the codebase and the `directory.json` routing are made via **Consensus-Based Development**:
* **Technical Updates:** Proposed via Public Pull Requests. Any change that compromises user privacy or neutrality is automatically rejected.
* **Directory Management:** Department email addresses are updated based on official government gazettes and CPGRAMS public notices.

## 3. Financial Model
SRO Unincorporated operates on a **Zero-Budget Model**:
* **Hosting:** Currently provided via community-supported or free-tier infrastructure (InfinityFree).
* **Labor:** All development and maintenance are performed on a voluntary, pro-bono basis by the citizen collective.

## 4. Conflict of Interest
SRO Unincorporated is independent of any political party or government department. Our role is strictly limited to providing a technical interface to existing government portals.

## 5. Contact & Accountability
In the absence of a physical office, accountability is maintained through:
* **Public Audit:** The entire codebase is open for inspection.
* **Primary Correspondent:** srounincorp@gmail.com
